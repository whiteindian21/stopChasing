Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 24, 2024

1. Introduction

Stop Chasing ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to our users. This includes:

  • Account Information: Your email address and password hash when you create an account.
  • Usage Data: Credits used and feature interactions to improve our service.
  • Conversation Content: Texts submitted for analysis are processed securely. These are only stored if you explicitly enable conversation history saving.
  • Payment Information: All transactions are handled securely by PayPal. We do not store your credit card numbers or bank details directly on our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • To manage your subscription and credit usage.
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • To monitor and prevent fraud or technical issues.

4. Data Storage & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Your conversations are private and encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell your personal data to third parties. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Services

We may employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our service:

  • PayPal: For processing subscription payments securely.
  • Hosting Providers: To host our application and database infrastructure.

These third parties have access to your Personal Information only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You may request the deletion of your account at any time via the Settings page. Upon account deletion, all stored conversations and personal data associated with your account are permanently removed from our active databases.

7. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Update your email and account information at any time.
  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request the deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Cancel your subscription at any time through your PayPal account or our Settings interface.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:
